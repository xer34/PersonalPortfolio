About Me

A full stack web developer with many years of experience as a paramedic and professional photographer. I thrive in high stress situations where decisive decision making and communication are paramount. I have been passionate about and all things tech since a very young age and have been building computers for work and play for over 20 years. I began my coding journey in 2002 with basic HTML and CSS.

The combination of web development, paramedicine and photography have made me a strong full stack developer. As a front end developer, the many years behind a camera have allowed me to develop an eye for what looks good. I truly believe that form should always follow function; as such the tools we use should be easy to use and intuitive. As a back end developer, I love tackling hard, logistical, and sometimes nebulous problems that sometimes present themselves. Practicing medicine over the years has had me find solutions to difficult situations daily, and is something I enjoy greatly.
